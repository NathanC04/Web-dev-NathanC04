How to make a Ham Sandwich

It's really easy to make a Ham Sandwich

You need a set of ingrediants to make a sandwich, including the following:

  • Bread
  • Ham
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce

Here is a step by step on how to make it:

  1. First, have to pieces of bread
  2. secondly, put one or two pices of ham on one of the bread
  3. Thirdly, add condiments on the ham, add cheese and lettuce
  4. Lastly, add the other piece of bread on top

This simple method will help you make a perfect ham sandwich. It's really simple and easy.
